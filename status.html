Status Checker Information

Welcome to the bot.html page. If you've arrived here, you've likely noticed our status checker accessing your website.

The only automated tool we use is GoExportStatusChecker/1.0, which simply verifies if your website is online.

This tool does not collect any data; it works as a companion to our main software, GoExport.

If you see this status checker in your server logs, it means a GoExport user has started the app, selected your website, and is exporting their video.
